## 1. Overview

Veresiye Defteri is a mobile application designed to help users manage customer debt, payment records, and simple account tracking.

This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user data.

## 2. Data Stored by the App

Veresiye Defteri allows users to enter and store the following information:

- Customer name
- Customer phone number
- Customer notes
- Debt records
- Payment records
- Transaction dates
- Transaction descriptions

This data is stored locally on the user’s device.

## 3. Local Storage

The app uses local device storage to save customer and transaction records.

Veresiye Defteri does not require an account and does not upload customer or transaction records to a remote server controlled by the developer.

If the user deletes the app or clears app data, locally stored records may be permanently deleted unless the user has exported a backup.

## 4. CSV Export and Import

The app allows users to export their records as a CSV file.

When users choose to export or share a CSV file, the selected sharing method may be handled by third-party apps installed on the device, such as email, messaging, cloud storage, or file sharing apps.

The developer is not responsible for how third-party apps process shared files after the user chooses to share them.

## 5. Advertising

Veresiye Defteri may display ads using Google AdMob.

Google AdMob may collect and process certain device, advertising, usage, and diagnostic data in accordance with Google’s own policies.

Users can review Google’s privacy practices here:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 6. Analytics and Crash Reporting

The app may use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand app usage, improve performance, and detect crashes.

These services may collect technical information such as:

- Device model
- Operating system version
- App version
- Crash logs
- Usage events
- Diagnostic information

This data is used only to improve app stability and user experience.

## 7. Notifications

The app may request notification permission to send reminders or app-related alerts.

Users can disable notifications at any time from their device settings.

## 8. Data Sharing

The developer does not sell user data.

Customer and transaction records entered into the app are not intentionally shared with the developer.

Data may be shared only when:

- The user manually exports or shares a CSV file
- Third-party services such as AdMob, Firebase, or the operating system process technical data
- Required by applicable law

## 9. Data Deletion

Users can delete their data directly inside the app by using the “Delete All Data” option.

Users may also delete the app from their device to remove locally stored data, depending on the operating system’s storage behavior.

## 10. Children’s Privacy

Veresiye Defteri is not intended for children under the age of 13.

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 11. Security

The app stores data locally on the user’s device. Users are responsible for protecting access to their device.

Although reasonable care is taken in app design, no storage method is completely secure.

## 12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Updates will be published in the same location where this Privacy Policy is hosted.
